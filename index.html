What is OpenStreetMap?

OpenStreetMap (OSM) is a collaborative project to create a free editable map of the world. It is recognized as a useful dataset for training machine learning algorithms, deriving analytics on mapping behaviors, providing vital datasets to first responders to natural disasters, and a casual place for geonerds to continue to contribute to a living map of the world.
